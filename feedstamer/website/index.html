Frequently Asked Questions

Which platforms does FeedsTamer support?

Currently, FeedsTamer supports Twitter/X, Instagram, YouTube, and LinkedIn. We're working on adding Reddit, Substack, and other platforms based on user demand.

How is this different from just following accounts on social media?

Traditional social media platforms use algorithms to show you content they think will maximize your engagement (and ad revenue), regardless of what you've chosen to follow. Feedstamer shows only content from accounts you've explicitly selected, in chronological order, with no algorithmic manipulation. Plus, our focus tools help you consume content intentionally rather than endlessly scrolling.

Is my data private and secure?

Yes, privacy is a core value at FeedsTamer. We only store the minimum data needed to provide our service, use end-to-end encryption, and never sell your information to third parties. You can export or delete your data at any time.

Can I interact with content through FeedsTamer?

Yes, you can like, comment, save, and share content directly through FeedsTamer. For more complex interactions, you can open the original post in the native app with one tap.

What happens when I reach my account limit on the free plan?

The free plan allows up to 10 accounts across all platforms. When you reach this limit, you'll need to remove an account before adding a new one, or upgrade to Premium for unlimited accounts.

Does FeedsTamer work offline?

Yes, FeedsTamer caches your recent Feeds for offline access. Premium users can customize offline storage settings and pre-download content for later reading.
